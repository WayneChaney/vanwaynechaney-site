## CREATIVE DIRECTION (SESSION NOTES 2026-06-20)

### The Core Concept
Show the PROBLEM before Wayne ever appears on screen. Multiple contractors missing calls because they're slammed — not lazy. Customer is on the other end typing "Ok nevermind." That's the leak made visible.

### The Emotional Hook — FOMO/Anxiety
Scene 2 plays differently now. While Wayne talks about "AI taking over the game," the viewer has ALREADY watched money walk out. The anxiety is:
- "This is happening to me right now"
- "My competitor probably already has this"
- "I'm losing jobs I don't even know about"

That's the real closer. Not features. Fear of being left behind.

### Scene 4 Visual Layer (upgraded)
Don't just show the Google Sheets dashboard. Intercut it with more "Ok nevermind" texts from different customers — different phones, different names. Each AI response fires back instantly. Customer surprised. Books. That contrast (ignored vs. instant response) is the whole pitch in 10 seconds.

### AI Visuals Strategy (pre-Fiverr rough cut)
Generate Shots A-D in Higgsfield first. Validate the concept looks right. THEN take to Fiverr for polish. Don't pay for edit until the rough version confirms the feeling is there.
