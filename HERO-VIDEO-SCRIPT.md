---

## RESEARCH-VALIDATED RULES (don't skip these)

- **First 3 seconds are everything.** 50-60% of viewers drop off in the first 3 seconds. Scene 1 must hit on frame one — no slow build.
- **Captions on every scene.** 71% of B2B video plays muted. Contractor opens this on his phone between jobs. No captions = lost.
- **Problem-first beats talking head.** Confirmed. Scene 1 before Wayne on camera is correct.
- **Real footage > AI for trade workers.** Use Pexels for contractor scenes. Use Higgsfield only for the "customer texting" sequence.
- **Screen recording demo builds more trust than animation.** Keep Scene 4 as-is.
- **CTA within 2 clicks.** "Book a Strategy Call" button stays.

## DELIVERY GUIDE (HOW TO RECORD)

### Scenes 2, 3, 5 — On Camera
Just talk. Natural pace, ~10% slower than normal conversation. Don't build in pauses — the editor controls that. Music plays under you the whole time. Say your lines with energy, editor breathes it out in post.

### Scene 4 — Voiceover Only (different — pause deliberately)
Each line needs a 2-second pause so editor can sync to the dashboard tab animations.

Tell the Fiverr editor: "Scene 4 voiceover has 2-second pauses between each line — sync each line to the next tab animating in."

## CREATIVE DIRECTION (SESSION NOTES 2026-06-20)

### The Core Concept
Show the PROBLEM before Wayne ever appears on screen. Multiple contractors missing calls because they're slammed — not lazy. Customer is on the other end typing "Ok nevermind." That's the leak made visible.

### The Emotional Hook — FOMO/Anxiety
While Wayne talks about "AI taking over the game," the viewer has ALREADY watched money walk out. The anxiety is:
- "This is happening to me right now"
- "My competitor probably already has this"
- "I'm losing jobs I don't even know about"

That's the real closer. Not features. Fear of being left behind.

### Scene 4 Visual Layer
Don't just show the Google Sheets dashboard. The contrast between the "Ok nevermind" texts in Scene 1 and the instant AI responses in Scene 4 is the whole pitch in 10 seconds.

### Why Real Pexels Footage for Scene 1
AI-generated contractor footage looks slightly off for trade work. Real Pexels clips = authentic. AI (Higgsfield) = only for the customer texting sequence where you need text overlay control and emotional narrative. Hybrid approach is the right call.
