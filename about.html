Who is Vanwayne Chaney Jr.?

I’m Wayne. I’m a software engineer, AI builder, and systems-minded operator from Cleveland, Ohio. I build AI workflows, digital tools, and business systems, but the real point of this page is simpler than that. It is to show you who I am, what shaped me, and why I work the way I do.

I care about building real relationships with real people, especially business owners and operators who are trying to tighten up their systems, save time, and stop losing money to sloppy follow-up or messy operations.

Through VC2, I focus on practical AI and operator tools that actually help. This is the background behind that: Cleveland roots, engineering discipline, a real interest in systems, and a builder mindset that comes from both work and life.

02

Mission: build useful systems for people doing real work.

I want the work to feel personal, practical, and local. Good systems should help good people move faster, follow up better, and run cleaner businesses.

I was born and raised in Cleveland, and I grew up around people who worked hard and had to figure things out without making a big show out of it. That shaped me more than anything else.

My father worked hard and did all the trades: electrical, plumbing, roofing, whatever needed to get done. Helping around that world taught me early that real work matters, details matter, and if you want a better future you have to build it.

I also grew up around my father's small real estate business, which meant I met a lot of local contractors and saw how much work happens behind the scenes. That stayed with me. It is a big reason I care about helping local businesses now.

Cleveland is home. My family is from here. I grew up on Browns games even when we lost, and I still remember how crazy it felt seeing the Cavs win in 2016 and the whole city come alive. So when I say I want to start local, I mean that for real.
