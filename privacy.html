How We Use Your Information

Information collected through our systems is used to:

We operate AI automation systems on behalf of trades and service businesses including electrical, HVAC, plumbing, roofing, and similar contractors. Your data may be shared with the business whose system you interacted with solely to fulfill your service request.

By providing your phone number and engaging with our system, you consent to receive SMS messages including appointment confirmations, follow-up reminders, and scheduling communications.

Message frequency varies based on your interaction and the service requested. You may receive multiple messages as part of a follow-up or reminder sequence.

Message and data rates may apply.

You may also receive follow-up messages related to your service inquiry, including lead qualification questions and review requests.

To opt out at any time, reply STOP to any message. To request help, reply HELP.

Contact and interaction data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the service request or as required by law. You may request deletion at any time.
