Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · vanwaynechaney.com

Information We Collect

When you schedule a service appointment through our AI receptionist, we collect the information you provide during the call: your name, phone number, service address, and appointment details. This information is collected solely to confirm and manage your appointment.

How We Use Your Information

Your phone number and appointment details are used exclusively to send you a single SMS confirmation message after your appointment is booked. We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose beyond appointment confirmation and service coordination.

SMS Messaging

By providing your phone number during a service call, you consent to receive one SMS confirmation message from us. Message and data rates may apply.

To opt out of SMS messages at any time, reply STOP to any message you receive. To request help, reply HELP.

Message frequency: One message per appointment booked.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Your information may be shared with the service business that booked your appointment solely to fulfill your service request.

We use Twilio to deliver SMS messages. Twilio's privacy policy governs how they handle message delivery data.

Data Retention

Appointment and contact information is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the service request. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. Contact us at vanwaynec01@gmail.com.
